What is ATS?

ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is automated software used by recruiters to screen resumes. Your score indicates how well your resume is optimized for these systems.

A higher score means better chances of passing initial resume screening.

Tips to improve:
  • Use standard fonts and formatting
  • Include relevant keywords from job descriptions
  • Use clear section headings
  • Avoid graphics, tables, and unusual layouts
